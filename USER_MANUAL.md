# JomBudget User Manual

JomBudget is a role-based student budget travel planner for Malaysia. The app supports three user roles — **Traveler**, **Vendor**, and **Admin** — each with a dedicated interface.

## 1. Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Open JomBudget on your device.
2. On the **Register** tab, fill in:
   - **Name** — your full name or business name
   - **Email** — a valid email address
   - **Password** — minimum 6 characters
   - **Role** — choose **Traveler** (for trip planning and booking) or **Vendor** (for listing travel services)
3. Tap **Create Account**.

> Admin accounts are created by the platform administrator and cannot be self-registered.

### Logging In

1. On the **Login** tab, enter your registered email and password.
2. Tap **Sign In**.
3. You will be directed to the home screen for your role.

### Logging Out

Tap your **Profile** tab and scroll to the bottom to find the **Logout** button.

## 2. Traveler Guide

Travelers can browse listings, book services, plan full itineraries, and manage their trips.

### 2.1 Browse

The Browse tab is your main discovery screen.

- **Search** — type keywords to filter listings by title, description, or tags.
- **Filter by State** — use the state dropdown to narrow results by Malaysian state.
- **Filter by Type** — filter by category: Accommodation, Restaurant, Activity, or Attraction.
- **Listing Cards** — each card shows the listing image, title, location, price per pax, and average rating.

**Viewing a Listing**

Tap any listing card to expand its detail view, where you can:

- View full description, tags, state, and pricing
- **See Reviews** — tap the Reviews button to read traveler ratings and comments, including vendor replies
- **See Q&A** — tap Q&A to read public questions and vendor answers
- **Ask a Question** — submit your own question to the vendor; choose to make it public or keep it private
- **Book Now** — opens the booking form (see below)

**Making a Booking**

1. Tap **Book Now** on any listing.
2. Select your **start date** and **end date** (same day is valid for day activities).
3. Choose the number of **pax** (1–6 people).
4. Select a **payment method**: Card, E-Wallet, or Online Banking.
5. Tap **Confirm Booking**.

> Dates that fall within a vendor's blackout window are automatically disabled and cannot be selected.

---

### 2.2 Planner

The Planner helps you build a full trip itinerary within your budget.

**Quick Presets**

Tap one of four preset buttons to auto-fill common trip profiles:
- **Budget Saver** — low cost, hostel, bus, relaxed pace
- **Balanced** — mid-range, mixed transport, balanced pace
- **Adventure** — activity-heavy, mixed transport, packed pace
- **Food Trail** — food and culture focused, balanced pace

**Manual Planning**

Fill in the trip parameters:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| Start City | Your departure city |
| Destination | Select a Malaysian state and a specific destination |
| Dates | Trip start and end dates |
| Budget (RM) | Your total available budget |
| Pace | Relaxed, Balanced, or Packed |
| Transport Mode | Bus, Train, or Mixed |
| Stay Type | Hostel, Budget Hotel, or Mid-Range |
| Interests | Select up to 8 tags (food, nature, culture, history, shopping, adventure, night-life, community) |

Tap **Generate Itinerary** to produce your plan.

**Reading Your Plan**

- **Cost Breakdown** — shows estimated spend by category: transport, accommodation, activities, food, and fees
- **Remaining Budget** — how much budget is left after the plan
- **Day-by-Day Schedule** — each item shows the day, time slot, listing, estimated cost, and notes
- **Cheaper Alternatives** — similar listings at a lower price point
- **Upgrade Picks** — premium options if your budget allows

**Destination Insights**

Below the planner form, each selected destination shows its typical budget range, recommended number of days, and highlight activities.

**Recent Plans**

Previously generated plans appear as templates at the bottom of the Planner screen. Tap any template to reload its parameters and regenerate.

---

### 2.3 Bookings

The Bookings tab lists all your past and upcoming bookings.

Each booking card shows the listing title, dates, number of pax, total amount, booking status, and payment status.

**Booking Status Types**

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Pending | Submitted, awaiting vendor confirmation |
| Confirmed | Vendor has accepted the booking |
| Completed | Trip has taken place |
| Cancel Requested | You have requested a cancellation |
| Cancelled | Booking has been cancelled |
| Rejected | Vendor declined the booking |

**Actions Available**

- **View Receipt** — tap any booking to open a detailed receipt dialog
- **Save Receipt** — download the receipt to your device
- **Share Receipt** — share the receipt via other apps
- **Request Cancellation** — available on Pending or Confirmed bookings
- **Mark as Completed** — available on Confirmed bookings once your trip is done
- **Leave a Review** — available on Completed bookings; submit a star rating (1–5) and a written comment

---

### 2.4 Notifications

The Notifications tab shows alerts from the platform such as booking confirmations, vendor responses to your questions, and admin announcements.

- Unread notifications are highlighted.
- Tap a notification to mark it as read.

---

### 2.5 Profile

The Profile tab displays your account information (name, email, role) and a summary of your travel activity including the number of trips planned and your average projected spend. Budget policy tips are also shown as reminders.

## 3. Vendor Guide

Vendors list their travel services on the platform, manage availability, respond to bookings and customer feedback, and track earnings.

### 3.1 Listings

The Listings tab shows all your published services.

**Creating a Listing**

Tap the **+** button to open the new listing form:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| Title | Name of your service |
| Description | Full description shown to travelers |
| Type | Activity, Accommodation, Restaurant, or Attraction |
| Location | Specific location name |
| State | Malaysian state |
| Base Price (RM) | Price per pax |
| Tags | Comma-separated keywords (e.g. beach, halal, family) |
| Photos | Upload one or more images from your device |

Tap **Save** to publish the listing.

**Editing a Listing**

Tap the edit icon on any listing card to modify its details. All fields can be updated after publishing.

**Pausing and Activating a Listing**

Use the toggle on each listing card to pause (hide from travelers) or reactivate a listing at any time.

**Managing Availability (Blackout Windows)**

Tap **Availability** on a listing card to open the blackout calendar.

- **Add Blackout Window** — select a date range and enter a reason (e.g. "Public holiday", "Maintenance")
- **Remove Blackout Window** — tap the delete icon next to any existing window
- Travelers cannot select dates that fall within a blackout window when booking.

---

### 3.2 Bookings

The Bookings tab lists all incoming bookings across your listings.

Each card shows the listing name, traveler details, dates, pax count, total amount, and payment status.

**Confirming or Rejecting a Booking**

Bookings that have been paid and are in **Pending** status show two action buttons:

- **Confirm** — accept the booking; the traveler is notified
- **Reject** — decline the booking; the traveler is notified

> Only paid bookings can be confirmed or rejected.

**Viewing a Receipt**

Tap any booking to open its receipt, which can also be saved or shared.

---

### 3.3 Earnings

The Earnings tab gives a snapshot of your financial performance:

- **Total Revenue** — sum of all paid confirmed and completed bookings
- **Confirmed Bookings** — count of accepted bookings
- **Completed Bookings** — count of fulfilled bookings
- **Performance Log** — your last 10 bookings with status and amount

---

### 3.4 Feedback

The Feedback tab is split into two sections.

**Traveler Questions (Q&A)**

Lists all questions submitted by travelers on your listings, both public and private.

- Questions with no reply show an **Answer** button.
- Questions you have already answered show an **Edit** button to update your reply.
- Tap either button, type your response in the dialog, and tap **Submit**.

**Reviews**

Lists all reviews left on your listings including the star rating, comment, and date.

- Tap **Reply** to write a response visible to all travelers on the listing.
- Existing replies show an **Edit** button.

---

### 3.5 Profile

Displays your account name, email, and role. Use this tab to log out.

## 4. Admin Guide

Admins oversee the entire platform — managing users, listings, destinations, bookings, reviews, and sending platform-wide announcements.

### 4.1 Users

The Users tab lists every registered account on the platform.

Each row shows the user's name, email, and role.

**Deactivating / Reactivating a User**

Toggle the switch next to any user to deactivate or reactivate their account. Inactive users cannot log in until reactivated.

---

### 4.2 Listings

The Listings tab shows every listing across all vendors.

- **Toggle Active** — pause or reactivate any listing on the platform.
- **Delete Listing** — tap the delete icon and confirm. Deletion is blocked if the listing has active bookings; resolve those bookings first.

---

### 4.3 Destinations

Destinations are curated location guides used by the Traveler Planner feature.

**Creating a Destination**

Tap **+** and fill in:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| Name | Destination name (e.g. George Town Heritage District) |
| State | Malaysian state |
| Description | Short summary shown to travelers |
| Budget Low / High (RM) | Typical per-trip budget range |
| Recommended Days | Suggested trip length |
| Highlights | Comma-separated activity tags |

**Editing a Destination**

Tap the edit icon on any destination card to update its details.

**Pausing / Deleting a Destination**

- Toggle the switch to hide a destination from the Planner without deleting it.
- Tap the delete icon and confirm to permanently remove it.

---

### 4.4 Bookings

The Bookings tab shows every booking on the platform with search and filter tools.

**Search**

Type in the search bar to filter by listing title, traveler ID, or vendor ID.

**Filter by Status**

Use the status filter to view: All, Pending, Confirmed, Completed, or Cancel-Requested bookings. A count badge shows how many bookings match each status.

**Overriding a Cancellation**

Bookings with **Cancel-Requested** status show an **Override Cancellation** button.

1. Tap **Override Cancellation**.
2. Optionally edit the cancellation reason (defaults to "Traveler changed plans").
3. Tap **Confirm** — the booking is cancelled, a mock refund is processed, and the traveler is notified.

**Viewing a Receipt**

Tap any booking to open its receipt dialog with full details.

---

### 4.5 Reviews & Reports

This tab contains platform moderation tools, analytics, inquiry management, and review oversight.

**Announcements**

Send a notification to platform users:

1. Enter a **title** and **message body**.
2. Select the **audience**: All Users, Travelers only, Vendors only, or Admins only.
3. Tap **Send Announcement** — the notification appears in the selected users' Notifications tabs.

**Report Snapshot**

An at-a-glance summary of platform health:

- Total and pending booking counts
- Total paid revenue (proxy metric)
- Most popular listings
- Breakdown of cancellation reasons

**Planner Analytics**

Statistics on traveler itinerary usage:

- Total planner runs
- Number of infeasible plans (over-budget requests)
- Average projected spend
- Top 3 destination states by popularity
- Last 5 generated plans with traveler, destination, budget, dates, and status

**Inquiry Management**

Lists all traveler questions submitted across the platform (public and private).

- Tap **Answer** or **Edit** on any inquiry to submit or update a response on behalf of the platform.

**Review Moderation**

Lists all reviews across all listings.

- Toggle the **Flag** switch on any review to mark it for follow-up.
- Flagged reviews are visually distinguished and can be monitored.

---

### 4.6 Profile

Displays the admin account name, email, and role label. The last six platform notifications are shown here and can be marked as read by tapping them. Use this tab to log out.
